Strawberry Cheesecake Cups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/3 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • Fresh strawberries (sliced)

Instructions

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter.
  2. Spoon the mixture into the bottom of small cups.
  3. In a bowl, beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Fold in whipped cream until smooth.
  5. Spoon cheesecake mixture over the crust layer.
  6. Top with fresh strawberries and chill before serving.
