Breakfast

Cinnamon Apple French Toast Skillet

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bread (thick sliced works best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-2 apples, sliced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1-2 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices into the mixture until coated.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook bread 2-3 minutes per side until golden.
  5. Add apples to the skillet and sprinkle with cinnamon and brown sugar.
  6. Cook apples until softened, then serve on top of the french toast.
