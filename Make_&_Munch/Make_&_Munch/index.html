Sunday Pot Roast

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lb beef chuck roast
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 3 potatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  1. Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. Season the beef chuck roast with salt, pepper, and thyme.
  3. Place the roast in a large roasting pan.
  4. Add carrots, potatoes, onion, and garlic around the roast.
  5. Pour the beef broth and worcestershire sauce over the roast.
  6. Cover the pan with a lid or foil.
  7. Cook in the oven for about 3-4 hours until the meat is tender.
  8. Remove from oven, let it rest for 10 minutes, then serve.
